Our transport division comprises 102 trucks available for hire. In order to remain flexible and successfully provide personalized services to our clients, we operate a fleet of side tippers, flatbed, and drop sides. The entire fleet is fully insured with goods in transit insurance covering the whole of Southern Africa. All our vehicles have a fully installed tracking system that is closely monitored 24 hours a day and works well throughout Southern Africa.

Services

The company provides road transport and logistics solutions within the country and between Zimbabwe and neighbouring Southern African countries such as South Africa, Zambia, Botswana and Namibia

Contact Us

Our Aim

Our aim is to provide affordable, one stop transport and logistical services for all types of road transportation within Southern Africa. We strive to be the most successful transport and logistics company in the regional industry and to continually improve our market share by offering competitive rates in conjunction with superior service, optimal use of available resources, innovation and cost saving initiatives.

The company aims to maintain and grow on its high profile in the market by:

  • Maintaining service excellence
  • Providing superior transport solutions according to each customer’s needs
  • Treating staff and customers with respect and honesty
  • Appointing high quality staff (vetted by extensive background checks)
  • Developing new and innovative ways to safely and cost effectively transport cargo
  • Offering customers a dedicated, personalised service

We currently have a contract with Mimosa Mining Company to transport ore concentrate to South Africa and other contracts with WFP, GMB and National Foods. We also have another contract with Food Reserve Agencies of Zambia transporting grain from Zambia to Gweru and are also offering transport services to several other businesses and organisations on hiring basis.
